Approach

Project Management Methodology

This project applies structured PM principles drawn from PRINCE2 and agile hybrid methodology combining a defined WBS and Gantt for planning rigour with iterative delivery cycles for content and development work.

Four top-level deliverable branches Strategy & Market Entry, Digital Innovation & AI, Organisational Behaviour & Society, and Website & PM Documentation decomposed into 16 discrete work packages.
