The Thinking
Behind the Work

I'm a Management Consultancy postgraduate at UCD Michael Smurfit, originally from New Delhi. I completed my B.Com in First Division (7.955/10) at Motilal Nehru College, University of Delhi, where I also led the Content & Research department of our Commerce Society and served as a Senior Associate at 180 Degrees Consulting.

Consulting chose me as much as I chose it. The moment that made it clear was working with 180DC on the RCED project—sitting across from an organisation that had spent 30 years training entrepreneurs and lifting communities, and realising that good strategy could directly amplify that work. That feeling has stayed with me.

Outside of strategy, I'm a committed Manchester City supporter and follow Indian cricket closely—big Delhi Capitals fan. When I'm not working through a market analysis, I'm watching a thriller film, following geopolitics and defence tech, or revisiting Sade or Kenny Loggins. I have a preference for high-stakes, slow-burn situations—which probably explains the career direction.
