I am a postgraduate student at UCD Michael Smurfit Graduate Business School, originally from New Delhi. My work sits at the intersection of structured analysis and real-world impact — from EV market entry strategies to CSR fundraising for underserved communities.

I completed my Bachelor of Commerce in First Division (7.955/10) at Motilal Nehru College, University of Delhi, where I also led the Content and Research department of our Commerce Society and served as a Senior Associate at 180 Degrees Consulting. Those experiences, particularly the RCED project, pulled me firmly towards consulting.

Consulting chose me as much as I chose it. The moment that made it clear was sitting across from an organisation that had spent 30 years training entrepreneurs in rural India and realising that good strategy could directly amplify that work. That feeling has stayed with me through every project since.
